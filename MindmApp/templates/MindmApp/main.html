Instructions on how to use MindmApp
  • Add a name to your MindmApp where it says 'MindmApp Name'. This How-To will keep appearing until you do so
  • Click on any of the node/neuron's to add an image from a dropdown
    menu that appears
  • If you run your mouse over the page, text areas will become visible. Feel free to add some text
  • On the left hand side of the page, two zoom icons appear. When the top one is clicked, the mindmap fills the page. The other icon reverts the zoom back to normal.
  • Your username appears at the top left. You can logout by clicking the Logout button at the top right
